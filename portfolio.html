Portfolio Solutions

Tailored delegated authority partnerships built on trust, expertise, and a shared commitment to underwriting excellence.

Delegated Authority

Partnership-Led Approach

AEGIS London's Portfolio Solutions team manages our delegated authority business, working closely with coverholders and managing general agents (MGAs) to deliver tailored insurance solutions across a wide range of product lines.

We take a partnership-led approach, investing time to understand each coverholder's unique capabilities, target markets, and growth ambitions. Our goal is to build long-term, mutually beneficial relationships that deliver sustainable, profitable portfolios.

Backed by the strength and stability of the AEGIS mutual, we offer coverholders the confidence of a financially secure capacity provider with a proven track record of commitment to the delegated authority market.

Our Solutions

What We Offer

Comprehensive delegated authority solutions tailored to your business needs.

Binding Authority Agreements

Bespoke binding authority contracts allowing coverholders to underwrite on behalf of Syndicate 1225, with clear guidelines and authority levels.

Line Slips & Consortia

Participation in line slips and consortium arrangements, providing additional capacity and market access through established lead underwriters.

Portfolio Monitoring

Robust MI reporting, performance analytics, and regular portfolio reviews to ensure sustainable profitability and alignment with strategy.

Global Reach

Coverage across multiple territories including the US, Canada, Europe, and Asia Pacific through our network of trusted coverholder partners.

Digital Integration

Technology-enabled partnerships leveraging our OPAL platform and digital trading capabilities for seamless data exchange and process efficiency.

Claims Support

Dedicated claims handling support with access to our experienced in-house claims professionals across all classes of business.
