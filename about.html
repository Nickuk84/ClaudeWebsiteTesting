About AEGIS London

A consistently profitable, top-quartile insurer in the Lloyd's market since 1999.

AEGIS London is a consistently profitable, top-quartile insurer in the Lloyd's market, underwriting a wide range of business classes through Syndicate 1225. Formed in 1999 and part of long-established mutual insurance business, AEGIS Insurance Services Ltd, we are trusted by clients and partners for our disciplined approach to decision making and collaborative culture.

Our unique mutual structure means we're focused entirely on what matters most: delivering exceptional underwriting and claims service. We combine the financial strength and stability of our mutual parent with the deep specialist expertise that the Lloyd's market is renowned for.
