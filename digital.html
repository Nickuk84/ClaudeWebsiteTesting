Insurance Trading

AEGIS London launched its digital trading capabilities in 2017, establishing itself as a leader in the modernisation of the Lloyd's market. Our commitment to innovation has driven the development of three core digital solutions that are transforming how insurance is traded.

With a dedicated team of 12 specialists, we combine deep insurance expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver faster, more transparent, and more efficient trading experiences for brokers, coverholders, and clients.
