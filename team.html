Our People

Experienced leaders and industry experts driving AEGIS London forward.

Management Team

Senior Leadership

Alex Powell

Chief Executive Officer

Alex became CEO of AEGIS London in July 2022, having served the previous seven years as Chief Underwriting Officer. He has been integral in achieving and maintaining the Syndicate's top quartile performance, with a strategic focus on performance, culture, digitalisation, and transformational use of data. Alex joined AEGIS London in 2000, having started his career more than 25 years ago in broking. He is a member of the Lloyd's Market Association Board and an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Insurance.

Matthew Yeldham

Chief Underwriting Officer

Matthew became Chief Underwriting Officer and Active Underwriter in July 2022, having previously served as Deputy CUO and Head of Casualty since 2010. He is responsible for the delivery of the underwriting business plan and is a member of the LMA CUO Committee. Prior to AEGIS London, Matthew was Head of International Insurance at Aspen and Active Underwriter of Syndicate 4711.

Marianne Harvey

Chief Operating Officer

Marianne oversees operational and IT transformation through digitalisation at AEGIS London. She joined in 2012 as Head of Internal Audit, having previously worked at PwC and Rothschild. Marianne is a member of the LMA Operations Committee, a trustee for the Lloyd's Benevolent Fund, and a fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales.

Adam O'Reilly

Chief Actuary

Adam heads the actuarial function at AEGIS London, including reserving, capital modelling, and pricing. He also oversees the Data Science and Analytics team. Adam joined the company in 2006 and holds a Master's degree from the University of Warwick. He is a Fellow of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries and serves as executive sponsor for Diversity, Equity and Inclusion.

Katie Wade

Chief Financial Officer

Katie is responsible for financial strategy, reporting, and planning at AEGIS London. She brings extensive experience in financial management within the Lloyd's market and is focused on maintaining the Syndicate's strong financial position and delivering value to stakeholders.

Rhic Webb

General Counsel & Company Secretary

Rhic leads the legal and company secretarial functions at AEGIS London. He joined the company in 2010, bringing experience in corporate law, governance, and regulatory compliance. He is responsible for all legal, governance, regulatory, and company secretarial matters.

Charity Bare

Chief Risk & Compliance Officer

Charity leads the risk and compliance function at AEGIS London. She was appointed to the role in 2025, bringing deep expertise in risk management and regulatory frameworks within the London insurance market.

Claire Parkinson

Chief Human Resources Officer

Claire leads the people and talent strategy at AEGIS London, including recruitment, development, and culture initiatives. She is committed to building a diverse and inclusive workplace that attracts and retains the best talent in the market.
