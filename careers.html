Why AEGIS London

A Different Kind of Workplace

We're big enough to make an impact, small enough that everyone matters.

Mutual Advantage

As part of a mutual, we're not driven by short-term shareholder demands. This means we can take a longer-term view on talent development and career progression.

Collaborative Culture

With around 200 people, our flat structure means you'll work closely with experienced professionals and senior leadership from day one.

Professional Growth

We invest heavily in professional development including CII qualifications, Lloyd's market training, and leadership programmes.

Global Exposure

Work across international markets from London and Toronto, gaining exposure to a diverse range of specialist insurance classes.

Wellbeing & Balance

Flexible and hybrid working arrangements, comprehensive benefits, employee assistance programmes, and a genuine commitment to work-life balance.

Diversity & Inclusion

We're committed to building a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace where everyone can bring their authentic self to work.
