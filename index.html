About Us

Mutual Strength.
Market Expertise.

AEGIS London is the London market operation of AEGIS (Associated Electric & Gas Insurance Services), one of the world's largest mutual insurers. Our unique mutual structure means we're focused entirely on what matters most: delivering exceptional underwriting and claims service.

We combine the financial strength and stability of our mutual parent with the deep specialist expertise that the Lloyd's market is renowned for.
