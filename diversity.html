Diversity & Inclusion

Our commitment to building a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace.

Our Commitment

AEGIS London is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace where every individual is valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their best. We believe that diversity of thought, background, and experience strengthens our business, drives better decision-making, and enables us to serve our clients and partners more effectively. Our commitment to diversity and inclusion is embedded in our culture, our policies, and our day-to-day practices.

We recognise that building a truly inclusive organisation requires ongoing effort, accountability, and a willingness to listen and learn. We are dedicated to creating an environment where all colleagues feel they belong, regardless of their age, disability, gender reassignment, marriage or civil partnership status, pregnancy and maternity, race, religion or belief, sex, or sexual orientation.

Equal Opportunities

AEGIS London is an equal opportunities employer. We are committed to ensuring that no applicant or employee receives less favourable treatment on the grounds of any protected characteristic, and that all employment decisions are based on merit, qualifications, and business needs. Our recruitment, selection, training, development, and promotion processes are designed to be fair, transparent, and free from bias.

We actively work to remove barriers to participation and advancement and to ensure that our workforce reflects the diverse communities in which we operate. We regularly review our policies and practices to identify opportunities for improvement and to measure our progress against our diversity objectives.

Inclusive Culture

We are committed to creating a culture of inclusion where all colleagues feel safe, supported, and encouraged to bring their whole selves to work. We invest in diversity and inclusion training for all employees, including unconscious bias awareness, and we support employee-led networks and initiatives that celebrate diversity and promote understanding across the organisation.

Our leadership team is accountable for championing diversity and inclusion at every level of the business. We regularly engage with our colleagues through surveys, focus groups, and open dialogue to understand their experiences and to identify areas where we can do more to support an inclusive working environment.

Industry Engagement

AEGIS London actively participates in industry-wide diversity and inclusion initiatives within the Lloyd's market and the broader insurance sector. We collaborate with industry bodies, charities, and networks to share best practice, support the development of diverse talent pipelines, and contribute to a more inclusive industry for all. We are proud to support programmes that encourage people from underrepresented groups to pursue careers in insurance and financial services.

Reporting and Accountability

We are committed to transparency in our diversity and inclusion efforts. We monitor and report on key diversity metrics, including gender pay gap data, and we set measurable objectives to track our progress over time. Any concerns about discrimination, harassment, or victimisation can be raised through our confidential reporting channels, and all reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly.

If you have questions about our diversity and inclusion policy, please contact us at enquiries@aegislondon.co.uk.
