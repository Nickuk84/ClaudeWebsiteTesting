Claims Philosophy

How It Works

Claims Process

Our streamlined process ensures your claim is handled with the expertise and speed it deserves.

Step 1

Notification

Submit your claim notification through your broker or directly via our claims team. We acknowledge receipt promptly and assign a dedicated claims handler with the right expertise for your class of business.

Step 2

Assessment & Investigation

Our claims handler reviews the policy terms, assesses coverage, and coordinates with loss adjusters or other experts as needed. We keep all parties informed throughout the investigation process.

Step 3

Evaluation & Decision

Based on the evidence gathered, we make a fair and timely decision on the claim. For complex claims, we work collaboratively with all stakeholders to ensure the best possible outcome.

Step 4

Settlement & Payment

Once agreed, we process payments promptly. We believe in honouring our commitments efficiently, which is central to our reputation as a trusted Lloyd's syndicate.
