Privacy Notice

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Introduction

AEGIS London ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This privacy notice explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data when you interact with us, use our website, or engage with our insurance services. We are a data controller for the personal data we process and are committed to complying with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

This notice applies to all personal data processed by AEGIS London, including data collected through our website, in the course of our underwriting and claims activities, and through our business relationships with brokers, coverholders, and other partners.

Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data depending on the nature of your interaction with us:

  • Identity data: name, title, date of birth, and gender.
  • Contact data: postal address, email address, and telephone numbers.
  • Professional data: job title, employer, and professional qualifications.
  • Insurance data: information relevant to underwriting, risk assessment, and claims handling, which may include financial information and, where necessary, health data or other special category data.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, operating system, and other technology identifiers collected when you visit our website.
  • Usage data: information about how you use our website.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes and rely on the corresponding legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract: to underwrite insurance policies, administer claims, and manage our contractual relationships with brokers and policyholders.
  • Legitimate interests: to manage our business operations, improve our services, conduct market analysis, prevent fraud, and maintain the security of our systems.
  • Legal obligations: to comply with regulatory requirements, including those imposed by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), the Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA), and Lloyd's of London.
  • Consent: where we send you marketing communications or process special category data where no other legal basis applies.

Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with third parties where necessary for the purposes set out in this notice. These may include our parent company AEGIS Insurance Services Ltd, Lloyd's of London, reinsurers, brokers, coverholders, claims handlers, legal advisors, regulatory authorities, and service providers who support our business operations. All third-party recipients are required to process your data in accordance with applicable data protection law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Where we transfer personal data outside the UK, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the Information Commissioner's Office.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, regulatory, accounting, or reporting requirements. For insurance-related data, retention periods are typically aligned with policy and claims lifecycles and applicable limitation periods, which may extend for several years after a policy has expired or a claim has been settled.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have a number of rights in relation to your personal data, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and to object to processing. You also have the right to withdraw consent at any time where consent is the legal basis for processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

If you are not satisfied with our response to your request, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
